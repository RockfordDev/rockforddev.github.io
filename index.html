About

Rockford Dev is a local group for software developers and enthusiasts in Rockford, Illinois. Founded in 2024, we strive to promote professional development, technical learning, and real human connection.

We meet on the third Wednesday of every month at 6 PM on the second floor of the Rockford Public Library Main Branch, in the Maker Lab.

Our meetups include educational talks, open discussions, and project showcases. Some past topics:

GraphVis Google AppScript The Fediverse Duplicate Customer Accounts Creating This Website OWASP Top 10 APK Reverse Engineering
